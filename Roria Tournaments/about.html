// Who We Are

👥 About Us

The team behind RBG Roria Tournaments — passionate trainers building the best competitive Pokémon Roria community.

Our Mission

RBG Roria Tournaments was created by trainers, for trainers. We believe every competitor deserves a fair, fun, and well-organized tournament experience — whether you're a seasoned veteran or stepping into your first bracket. Our goal is to make Roria's competitive scene the most active and welcoming it has ever been.
